Frequently Asked Questions

What is web hosting?

Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and businesses to publish a website or web application on the internet. When you subscribe to a hosting service, you're renting space on a server that stores all your website's files and data.

What are the features of Hostinger hosting?

Hostinger hosting features include: lightning-fast loading speed, complete security, free domain, unlimited free SSL certificates, regular backups, 24/7 technical support, user-friendly control panel, and a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Can I migrate my existing website to Hostinger?

Yes, you can easily migrate your existing website to Hostinger. Hostinger provides a free and automatic website migration service, where their team of experts handles everything and keeps your website running throughout the process without any data loss.

Do I need technical knowledge to create a website on Hostinger?

No, you don't need technical knowledge to create a website on Hostinger. Hostinger provides an easy-to-use website builder with ready-made professional templates, as well as one-click WordPress installation, making website creation easy even for beginners.

Can I get a free domain with Hostinger hosting?

Yes, Hostinger offers a free domain for a full year when you subscribe to any hosting plan. After the first year, you can renew the domain at competitive prices.

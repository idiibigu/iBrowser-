Remote Technical Support Service

We provide remote technical support using AnyDesk software, allowing our technical team to securely and quickly access your device to solve technical problems without the need for a field visit. This service saves time and effort and ensures a quick solution to your problems.

Steps to Connect with Technical Support:

1

Download AnyDesk

Download AnyDesk from the official website and install it on your device.

2

Get Your ID Number

After running the program, you will see a screen containing a unique 9-digit identification number. This is the number you will need to share with us.

3

Fill Out the Request Form

Fill out the form available on this page with your information and your AnyDesk ID number.

4

Wait for Support Team Contact

The technical support team will contact you via WhatsApp and will ask you to confirm the connection when they try to access your device via AnyDesk.
